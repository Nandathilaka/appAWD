Examination Branch


The Assistant Registrar and her staff are constantly engaged in offering services to students for Examination & Registration purposes.

Examinations & Registration Division is located at the 4th Floor of West Wing of University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC) Building Complex.

Only Students who have valid registration of the UCSC are enrolled to evaluate their academic excellence. Â  They will normally be allowed to attend to such activities during working days from 8.30 am. to 4.15 pm.

The UCSC is affiliated to University of Colombo (UoC) for the purpose of awarding degrees. Therefore, the Vice-Chancellor of the University of Colombo is the principal executive officer and the principal academic officer who shall exercise all such powers vested in him by the Senate and the University Council to ensure uniformity and conformity with university standards for awarding degrees for the UCSC students.


Activities


    Every student of the University of Colombo School of Computing will be given a student Identity Card.

    Admission of such students selected by the University Grant Commission (UGC) for undergraduate courses of study in Computer Science and Information & Communication Technology.

    Conduct Internal/External undergraduate examinations & Postgraduate examinations.

    Conduct Award Ceremonies for award of certificates for BIT (External) Degree  Programme.

    Attend to activities of the External Degree Convocation.

    Issue of Academic Transcripts required by the all registered students of UCSC.
